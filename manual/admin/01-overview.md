# Admin Overview

## Purpose of the Admin role
The **Admin** manages data for one or more assigned companies. This role handles daily operations, users, and customer records within their scope.

## Where you work
You will work in the **Admin Panel**. Your team will provide the exact URL for your environment.

## Typical responsibilities
- Manage users within assigned companies.
- Maintain customers, agents, and site equipment.
- Create and track service orders.
- Coordinate with vendors and technicians.

## What you can access
Admins can only see data for their assigned companies. If you cannot see a section, contact your system administrator.

## Recommended workflow
1. **Confirm company data**
   - Review customer and site records.

2. **Set up users**
   - Create and manage user accounts for your company.

3. **Operate daily work**
   - Create, assign, and track service orders.

## Need help?
If you see missing access or incorrect company data, contact your system administrator.
