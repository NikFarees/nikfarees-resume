# Login and First Setup

Once your account is approved and your password is set, you can log in and complete your first setup.

## Step 1: Open SwatHub Portal

Go to the SwatHub staging portal:

https://staging.swataircond.com.my/

## Step 2: Log in as Vendor

1. Click **Vendor Login**
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click **Sign In**

## Step 3: Complete Your Profile

After logging in, review your profile details and ensure your company information is correct.
If any information is missing or incorrect, update it before proceeding.

## Step 4: Check Your Dashboard

You should now see the Vendor Dashboard.
Use the menu to explore available features and complete any required setup tasks.

## Need help?

If you cannot log in, reset your password or contact the administrator for assistance.
