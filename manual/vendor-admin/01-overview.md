# Vendor Admin Overview

## Purpose of the Vendor Admin role
The **Vendor Admin** manages a single vendor organization and its day-to-day operations. This role focuses on vendor-specific data such as service orders, technicians, teams, and vendor staff access.

## Where you work
You will work in the **Admin Panel**. Your team will provide the exact URL for your environment.

## Typical responsibilities
- Manage vendor staff accounts and access.
- Maintain vendor branches and technician teams.
- Create, assign, and track service orders for your vendor.
- Review pricing and catalog data that applies to your vendor.

## What you can access
Your access is limited to your vendor company and its branches. If you cannot see a section, contact your system administrator.

## Recommended workflow
1. **Confirm vendor profile**
   - Verify your vendor company details and branches.

2. **Set up people**
   - Add vendor staff and assign roles.
   - Maintain technician profiles and teams.

3. **Operate daily work**
   - Create and manage service orders.
   - Assign technicians and track progress.

4. **Review pricing**
   - Reference pricing tables and catalog items when creating orders.

## Need help?
If you see missing access or incorrect vendor data, contact your system administrator.
