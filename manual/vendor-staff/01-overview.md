# Vendor Staff Overview

## Purpose of the Vendor Staff role
The **Vendor Staff** role focuses on executing assigned work and updating service orders. Access is limited to the vendorâ€™s scope and, in many cases, specific branches.

## Where you work
You will work in the **Admin Panel**. Your team will provide the exact URL for your environment.

## Typical responsibilities
- View assigned service orders.
- Update order status and notes.
- Coordinate with your Vendor Admin or dispatch.

## What you can access
Vendor Staff can only see records assigned to their vendor and, when applicable, their branch.

## Need help?
If you cannot see a service order you expect, contact your Vendor Admin.
