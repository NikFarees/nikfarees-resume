## Description

A comprehensive multi-vendor air conditioning service management platform that centralizes service orders from multiple vendors into a unified system. The platform streamlines team assignment processes, manages service workflows, and provides role-based access control for vendors, administrators, and service teams. Built to improve operational efficiency and service coordination across the air conditioning industry.



## Key Features

### Multi-Vendor Management
- Centralized service order management across multiple vendors
- Vendor onboarding and approval workflow
- Company and branch hierarchy management

### Intelligent Team Assignment
- Automated service order routing to vendor teams
- Team capacity and availability tracking
- Dynamic task allocation based on service requirements

### Role-Based Access Control
- **Super Admin** - System configuration and vendor oversight
- **Vendor Admin** - Company management and staff administration
- **Vendor Staff** - Service operations and order management

### Administrative Dashboard
- Real-time service order tracking
- Performance metrics and analytics
- Vendor and team management interfaces
- Comprehensive reporting capabilities
