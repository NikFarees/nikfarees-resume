## Description

A full-stack web application for driving school management with automated license booking, scheduling, and payment processing for students, instructors, and administrators.

## Problem Statement

Manual paper-based operations in driving schools lead to:
- Inefficient scheduling and booking processes
- Difficulty tracking student progress
- Manual payment processing prone to errors
- Poor communication between stakeholders

## Solution

DriveFlow provides a centralized digital platform that:
- Automates license booking and scheduling
- Tracks student progress and certifications
- Processes payments digitally
- Streamlines communication between all parties

## Key Features

### For Students
- Online lesson booking
- Progress tracking dashboard
- Payment history and receipts
- Instructor availability view

### For Instructors
- Schedule management
- Student records access
- Attendance tracking

### For Administrators
- Complete system oversight
- Financial reporting
- User management
- Configuration settings
